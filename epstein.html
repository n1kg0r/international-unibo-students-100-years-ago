Boris Epstein (1892 — unknown)
Studied Medicine and Surgery at the University of Bologna
Russia
BIOGRAPHY


Boris Epstein, son of Massimo and Theodosia, was born on 7th of December in 1892 in Moscow, Russian Empire. He completed the Faculty of Medicine at the Imperial University of Moscow on April 5th of 1916 and was proclaimed Doctor of Medicine[1]. During the last two years of his degree, he became an assistant at the Imperial Institute of Experimental in Petrograd (Saint Petersburg).
Read more
When WWI (1914-1918) started, Boris did military service in the Moscow hospitals as a doctor[2]. In 1917, he kept on doing military service as a physician in the Caucasus Army, [3] which was the Russian field army that fought in the Caucasus Campaign and Persian Campaign; he stayed there until 1919.


In 1919 Boris Epstein left Russia and moved to Italy “because of the living conditions in Russia and the consequent bad health”[4]. Italy and other countries had just closed their consulates in Russia as a result of the difficult political situation. The October Revolution of 1917 was held in which the Bolshevik Party seized power in Russia, inaugurating the Soviet regime. Then the Russian Civil war started and lasted from 1917 to 1923. Thus, Boris could not follow usual procedures in order to legalize Russian documents there, including his Diploma.

In 1921, Boris Epstein sent a letter to the Rector of the University of Bologna asking to examine him in order to get a Degree in Medicine and Surgery. However, he could not enroll at the University without a legalized Diploma in the Italian Consular Authority in Russia. This soon became the main problem. He wrote a letter to a Rector of the University of Bologna, in which he explained this difficult situation. As a result of the correspondence between Boris Epstein, the Rector and other Italian authorities, the Ministry of Education in Rome permitted the University of Bologna to examine Boris Epstein. On the 28th April of 1922 Boris successfully passed the examination for the degree of Doctor of Medicine and Surgery, reporting grades 110/110 (one hundred and ten out of one hundred and ten).[5] In addition, the Ministry of Education entrusted the Ministry of Foreign Affairs with checking the authentication of Russian Diploma[6]. “The Russian Consulate in Rome could endorse the diploma of degree obtained under the tsarist regime. The legalization office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs would then authenticate the signature of the Russian Consul ".[7] Thus, his Russian Diploma was legalized and translated by an Italian translator. The translation was approved on the 16th September 1922. At that point, the correspondence between Boris Epstein and authorities stopped and since then Boris’ traces were progressively lost.

Faculty of Medicine and Surgery
Dissertation candidate Mr. Dr. Epstein Boris
Dissertation title . Pathogenesis of diabetes insipid .
Supervisor-Prof. Viola.
ORAL THESIS.
1. Diabetic eruptions.
2. Nutritional disorders exist in the breastfed infant due to altered qualitative and quantitative ratio of milk components.
3. By what stimuli is gastric juice secretion excited, according to Pavlow, and what is the mechanism of said excitation.
R. UNIVERSITÀ DI BOLOGNA
Facoltà di Medicina e Chirurgia
Candidato all’esame di Laurea Sig. Dott. Epstein Boris
Titolo della dissertazione . Patogenesi del diabete insipido .
Relatore-Prof. Viola.
TESINE ORALI
1. Eruzioni diabetiche.
2. Nel lattante al seno esistono disturbi della nutrizione dovuti a rapporto alterato qualitativo e quantitativo dei componenti del latte.
3. Da quali stimoli viene eccitata, secondo Pawlow, la secrezione del succo gastrico e qual è il meccanismo di detta eccitazione.
